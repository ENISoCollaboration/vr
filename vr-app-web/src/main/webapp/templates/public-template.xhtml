ENISo Info

This is ENISo Computer Engineering Departement's (ii) Web Site. Please don't forget to refer to ENISo's official website

ENISo (National Engineering School of Sousse) is a Tunisian public institution of higher education and research in the field of engineering. ENISo delivers 3 Engineering and 9 Master’s programs in the fields of Industrial Electronics (Industrial Electronics Department), Applied Informatics (Industrial Computing Department) and Mechatronics (Advanced Mechanics Department). Similarly to the French Grandes Ecoles, admission to ENISo is based on students' raking in a national competitive exam that students take after two years of intensive preparation in mathemathics and physics.

ENISo has two research laboratories in the fields of Mechanical Engineering and Electrical Engineering and is submitting for a third laboratory in Telecommunications And Embedded Systems. ENISo has been involved in several europeen programs on education and research such as PARENIS, Icré@, OSMOSE and TEMPUS. ENISo is also a member of the Mediterranean Engineering School’s Network (RMEI)
